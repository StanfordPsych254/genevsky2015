Thank you for doing this task! In this task, we aim to replicate the first study of Genevsky & Knutson, 2015.

According to the study, MTurk users' emotion ratings about the faces of individuals requesting money on a microloan (small loans crowdfunded by Internet users) site could be used to predict how quickly those microloans were funded, even though the MTurk users were completely independent of the users on the microloan site. This suggests that emotions that people express in photos may lead to differences in lending at the market level.
